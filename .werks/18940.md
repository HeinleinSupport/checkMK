Builds for SLES15 SP5 are no longer delivered.

If you are running Checkmk on SLES15 SP5, you will no longer receive updates or new builds for this platform. This may affect the support and security of your installation. You must migrate your Checkmk installation to a supported platform version to continue receiving updates and support.
